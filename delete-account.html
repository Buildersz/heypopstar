Popstar - Delete Your Account & Data

This page explains how to delete your account and data associated with the Popstar app.

How Popstar sign-in works

Popstar does not maintain its own separate user account system. Sign-in is handled entirely through your Google Account, used only to access YouTube search and playback on your behalf.

How to delete your data

Step 1: Revoke Popstar's access to your Google Account

Go to myaccount.google.com/permissions, find "Popstar" in your list of connected apps, and remove its access. This immediately revokes Popstar's ability to use your Google account for search and sign-in.

Step 2: Delete locally stored app data

Popstar stores your favorites, recent searches, and song queue locally on your device only - this data is never sent to our servers. To delete it, uninstall the Popstar app from your device, or clear the app's storage via your device's Settings > Apps > Popstar > Storage > Clear Data.

Step 3: Party Mode data

If you've hosted or joined a Party Mode session, that session's data (queued songs, singer names, scores) is stored temporarily and is automatically deleted from our servers when the host ends the party. No Party Mode data is retained after a session ends.

What data is deleted vs. retained

  • Deleted immediately: Locally stored favorites, search history, and queue (upon uninstall/clear data). Party Mode session data (automatically, when a party ends).
  • Not retained by Popstar: We do not maintain a separate database of user accounts or personal information beyond what is described above.
  • Managed by Google: Your Google Account sign-in and permissions are managed directly by Google, not by Popstar. Revoking access as described in Step 1 above stops Popstar from accessing your Google Account entirely.
