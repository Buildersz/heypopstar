Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

🔒 Short version: Popstar does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Your YouTube account credentials are never seen or stored by us. All voice processing happens on your device or through third-party APIs you authorize.

1. Who We Are

Popstar is an AI-powered karaoke assistant that runs on a Raspberry Pi device. This privacy policy explains how the Popstar software and hardware handles your data.

2. What Data We Collect

Popstar collects the minimum data necessary to function:

  • YouTube OAuth tokens — stored locally on your Popstar device only. We never see or store your Google credentials.
  • Voice audio — recorded briefly when you say "Hey Popstar." Processed locally (Vosk) and/or sent to Google Speech-to-Text for recognition. Audio is not stored.
  • Voice commands — the text of your commands is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI to understand your intent. Not stored beyond the current session.
  • Song queue and history — stored only in memory on your device. Cleared when Popstar restarts.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your Google account password or login credentials
  • Your YouTube watch history or personal YouTube data
  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Payment information
  • Any data sent to our servers (Popstar has no central server)

4. YouTube and Google

Popstar uses the YouTube Data API to search for karaoke videos. You authorize this access by signing into your own Google account through the device setup screen. This access is governed by:

  • Google's Privacy Policy
  • YouTube's Terms of Service

Popstar only requests read-only access to search YouTube. We do not post, modify, or delete anything on your YouTube account.

You can revoke Popstar's access to your Google account at any time by visiting myaccount.google.com/permissions.

5. Voice Processing

When you speak to Popstar, your voice is processed in two ways:

  • Wake word detection — processed entirely on your device using Vosk, an offline speech recognition model. No audio leaves your device for this step.
  • Command recognition — after the wake word is detected, your command audio is sent to Google Speech-to-Text for transcription. Google's privacy policy applies to this data.
  • Intent understanding — the text of your command is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI to determine what you want Popstar to do. Anthropic's privacy policy applies.

6. Third-Party Services

Popstar uses the following third-party services. Please review their privacy policies:

  • Google Speech-to-Text — voice transcription
  • Anthropic Claude — command understanding
  • YouTube Data API — karaoke video search

7. Data Security

All OAuth tokens are stored locally on your Popstar device. We recommend keeping your device physically secure. If you sell or give away your Popstar device, factory reset it by deleting the file ~/popstar/youtube_tokens.json.

8. Children's Privacy

Popstar is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

10. Contact Us
